✅ Option Selling Guide

Options trading me **Option Selling (Writing Options)** ek advanced strategy hai jisme Trader premium earn karta hai market ko trade kar ke. Yeh strategy **time decay aur probability advantage** par based hai.  

✅ Basic Concept
- Option seller = Premium receive karta hai by selling Call ya Put  
- Obligation hoti hai underlying ko buy/sell karne ki, agar option exercise ho jaye  
- Risk theoretically unlimited (Call) ya high (Put)  
- Profit limited to premium received  

✅ Step by Step Option Selling

1️⃣ **Select the Option Type**
- Bullish market → Sell Put Option  
- Bearish market → Sell Call Option  
- Neutral market → Sell ATM/OTM option for premium  

2️⃣ **Select Strike Price**
- OTM (Out of the Money) preferred → High probability expire worthless  
- Avoid deep ITM → Risk high, reward limited  

3️⃣ **Select Expiry**
- Short-term → High theta decay, premium earn fast  
- Medium-term → Balance between decay and risk  

4️⃣ **Check Greeks**
- Theta → Seller ke liye biggest advantage (time decay)  
- Delta → Price sensitivity, ideally low delta sell karo  
- Vega → Volatility increase risk ho sakta hai  

5️⃣ **Entry & Exit**
- Entry → Market trend + range-bound + premium attractive  
- Exit → Profit target reached, or underlying moves against  

✅ Notebook Example Table

| Option Type | Strike Type | Expiry     | Entry Condition                       | Exit Condition                     |
|------------|-------------|------------|--------------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Call Sell  | OTM         | 1-5 days   | Price below resistance, range bound  | Premium target hit or stop loss    |
| Put Sell   | OTM         | 1-5 days   | Price above support, range bound     | Premium target hit or stop loss    |
| Call Sell  | ATM/OTM     | 3-10 days  | Volatility high, premium attractive  | Stop loss if price breakout       |
| Put Sell   | ATM/OTM     | 3-10 days  | Volatility high, premium attractive  | Stop loss if price breakdown      |

✅ Important Notes
- Option selling me **risk high** hota hai → proper stop loss set karo  
- Always monitor Greeks, specially **theta** aur **vega**  
- Use only small portion of capital per trade  
- Avoid selling naked options if beginner  
- Profitable strategy over long-term, discipline aur consistency se
