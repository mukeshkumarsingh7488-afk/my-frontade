✅ Option Hedging Strategies

Option Hedging ka maksad **existing positions ka risk kam karna** aur market ke adverse moves se protection lena hai. Ye professional Traders aur institutions me commonly use hota hai.  

✅ Basic Concept
- Hedging = Risk management technique  
- Objective: Profit ko protect karna ya loss ko minimize karna  
- Instruments: Options (Calls, Puts), Futures  

✅ Common Hedging Techniques

1️⃣ **Protective Put**
- Hold underlying stock / asset  
- Buy put option → downside protection  
- Loss limited to premium paid for put  

2️⃣ **Covered Call**
- Hold underlying stock  
- Sell call option → extra premium income  
- Upside capped, downside unhedged  

3️⃣ **Collar Strategy**
- Hold stock  
- Buy protective put + Sell call  
- Loss limited + Premium partially offsets put cost  

4️⃣ **Straddle Hedge**
- Buy call & put at same strike (long straddle)  
- Hedge against volatility in either direction  
- Best for uncertain market events  

5️⃣ **Spread Strategies**
- Bull Call Spread → Buy lower strike call, sell higher strike call  
- Bear Put Spread → Buy higher strike put, sell lower strike put  
- Limited risk + limited reward  

✅ Notebook Example Table

| Hedging Strategy      | Position Type          | Objective                       | Key Notes                          |
|----------------------|---------------------|--------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Protective Put       | Long Stock + Long Put | Downside protection             | Loss limited, premium paid         |
| Covered Call         | Long Stock + Short Call| Generate income, limited upside | Upside capped, risk remains        |
| Collar               | Long Stock + Put + Call | Limited risk + offset cost     | Requires premium management       |
| Long Straddle        | Call + Put           | Hedge against volatility        | Costly, use near major events     |
| Bull/Bear Spreads    | Call/Put Spread     | Limited risk + limited reward  | Defined risk & reward structure   |

✅ Important Notes
- Hedging me **premium cost** aur **expiry date** consider karo  
- Volatility ka impact samajhna zaruri hai  
- Hedging se profit limit ho sakta hai, lekin risk majorly reduce hota hai  
- Multiple legs ko samajh kar hi trade karo  
- Hamesha market conditions aur trend check karo before hedging
